01 — About

Crafting Digital Experiences

I'm a WordPress Developer & AWS Cloud Engineer with 5+ years of experience building enterprise-grade CMS solutions and production-ready cloud infrastructure. My work combines deep ACF expertise with AWS architecture to deliver sites that editors love and servers can handle at scale.

I specialise in ACF-powered content systems — Flexible Content layouts, Repeater fields, Options Pages, and ACF Blocks — enabling non-technical teams to manage thousands of pages independently. From custom post types to relationship fields, I architect CMS ecosystems that grow with your business.

On the infrastructure side, I design multi-tier AWS environments with EC2, ALB, ASG, and VPC for high-availability, auto-scaling hosting. Combined with Linux server hardening, Core Web Vitals optimisation, and CDN integration, I ensure sites stay fast, secure, and reliable.

Certified as an AWS Solutions Architect and Red Hat Engineer, I bridge the gap between powerful CMS solutions and robust, scalable cloud infrastructure.
